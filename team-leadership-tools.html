1. 3-Day Onboarding Plan

This structured 3-day onboarding plan introduces new IT staff to policies, systems, team structure, and KPIs such as First Contact Resolution (FCR) and Uptime. It is designed to ease integration into tech environments and ensure new hires can start contributing meaningfully from Day 1.

By Day 3, practical ticket handling and feedback loops are embedded to reinforce performance expectations and clarity. Industry tools like Zendesk and Loom are used to create interactive learning, while knowledge-sharing platforms promote sustained, self-paced development.
